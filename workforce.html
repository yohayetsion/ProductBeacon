The Workforce

A workforce you govern. Here is everything it can do.

A workforce you govern should be one you can inspect. So the whole thing is on this page: every team, every specialist, and the work each one hands you. It arrives finished, in hours instead of weeks, and the decisions stay yours.

15 teams · 96 specialists, with Legal & Compliance under supervised terms · 250 skills · 120 libraries behind every deliverable

You commission the work You review what comes back You hold the line where it matters

Here is what you ask for, and here is what comes back.

The reason it arrives finished is not that the work is shallow. Behind every deliverable sits a specialist working from a proven method, 250 ready-made skills and 120 libraries of frameworks and hard-won patterns. So the output comes out shaped like work a professional would hand you, not a rough first draft you have to rebuild.

The premise is simple: the workforce produces, you govern. Every row below names where the call stays yours.

Launch plan

You bring

The product, the date you want, and what you already know about your market.

It runs the method

Readiness gates, channel and messaging plan, a pre-mortem on what could sink the launch, then the day-one and week-one motion.

Drawing on

Contract triage

You bring

The contract and what the deal is supposed to do.

It runs the method

A structured first pass: clause-by-clause flags, severity on each finding, the questions your lawyer will want answered, and a list of what it deliberately did not opine on.

What lands

A triage brief your own lawyer reviews and signs off on. The handoff to a qualified human is not a caveat. It is the last step of the method.

Drawing on

The contract-law library. Drafting and triage only, never legal advice.
