The Workforce

A workforce you govern. Here is everything it can do.

Everyone wants to talk about how many agents you get. Here is the question that actually matters: what shows up on your desk, and who decides whether to trust it? This page is the answer. Not a roster. A catalogue of finished, professional-grade work, the kind that used to take a department to produce, arriving in hours instead of weeks, with you governing every call that matters.

~100 specialists, with Legal & Compliance under supervised terms · 130+ skills · 50+ libraries behind every deliverable

You commission the work You review what comes back You hold the line where it matters

Here is what you ask for, and here is what comes back.

The reason it arrives finished is not that the work is shallow. Behind every deliverable sits a specialist working from a proven method, well over a hundred ready-made skills and more than fifty libraries of frameworks and hard-won patterns. So the output comes out shaped like work a professional would hand you, not a rough first draft you have to rebuild.

The premise is simple: the workforce produces, you govern. Every row below names where the call stays yours.
