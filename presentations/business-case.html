1. Executive Summary

1. Executive Summary

ProductBeacon is a product services brand that combines 17 years of enterprise product leadership (NICE, Cognyte, $200M+ portfolios) with a proprietary AI operating system (Product Org OS: 82 agents, 101 skills, Vision to Value methodology). The business targets venture-backed startups and scaling companies that need senior product leadership but cannot justify a $300K+ full-time CPO hire or a $500K+ consulting engagement.

The opportunity is driven by three converging forces: the fractional executive market reaching $5.7B with 14% annual growth, AI consulting projected to grow from $11B to $91B by 2035 (26.2% CAGR), and 30% of midsize enterprises expected to have a fractional executive by 2027. ProductBeacon sits at the intersection of these trends, creating a new category -- AI-Augmented Product Leadership -- where a single senior operator, amplified by AI, delivers the output traditionally requiring a team.

Two completed engagements validate the model: AXIA (8 weeks, $106K equivalent value across 9 work streams) and Legionis (full E2E build from concept to near-launch). The core thesis is that AI amplification of a senior product leader creates a fundamentally different unit economics profile than traditional consulting, enabling premium outcomes at accessible price points.

Recommendation: GO -- with phased investment in brand, sales infrastructure, and case study documentation before scaling outreach.

The fractional executive population doubled from 60,000 to 120,000 between 2022 and 2024. This is not a trend -- it is a structural shift in how companies access senior talent.

2.2 Why Now

Three forces are converging simultaneously:

  1. AI maturity has crossed the utility threshold. Large language models, agent frameworks, and AI-powered workflows have moved from experimental to production-ready. A single operator with the right AI infrastructure can now produce work that previously required 3-5 people. ProductBeacon's Product Org OS is that infrastructure -- built, tested, and proven across real engagements.
  1. Fractional leadership has gained mainstream acceptance. The stigma of "part-time executive" has dissolved. Boards, VCs, and founders now actively seek fractional leaders as a capital-efficient strategy. The 30% midsize enterprise adoption prediction for 2027 reflects demand that is already materializing.
  1. The product leadership gap is acute. Venture-backed startups need strategic product leadership to deploy capital effectively, but the talent pool of experienced CPOs/VPs is shallow and expensive. The gap between "what founders can afford" and "what the business needs" is exactly where ProductBeacon operates.

3.4 Scalability Path

3.4 Scalability Path

ProductBeacon has a natural scaling trajectory:

Phase 1: Solo Operator (Year 1) Yohay delivers all engagements personally, amplified by Product Org OS. Revenue is capped by personal capacity. Focus: prove the model, build case studies, refine pricing.

Phase 2: Curated Network (Year 2-3) Select 2-3 senior product leaders as affiliates/subcontractors. Each is trained on Product Org OS and Vision to Value methodology. Yohay leads flagship engagements, network handles overflow. Revenue model shifts to include a margin on network engagements.

Phase 3: Methodology Licensing (Year 3+) Product Org OS and Vision to Value become licensable assets. Revenue diversifies: services (high-touch) + licensing (scalable). This path naturally connects to Legionis (the AI workforce platform) as the technology commercialization vehicle.

5.3 Sensitivity Analysis

The model is most sensitive to these inputs (in order of impact):

  1. Concurrent engagement capacity -- Each additional concurrent client multiplies revenue linearly. The question: can Yohay serve 2, 3, or 4 clients simultaneously at quality?
  1. Average deal size -- The difference between $25K/mo (leadership only) and $50K/mo (leadership + execution) is a 2x revenue multiplier on the same capacity.
  1. Utilization rate -- A realistic services business runs at 60-75% utilization (accounting for sales, admin, bench time, holidays). Overestimating utilization is the most common services business mistake.
  1. Client retention/duration -- A 3-month engagement requires 4x the pipeline of a 12-month engagement to hit the same annual revenue.

5.4 Break-Even Framework

Break-Even = Fixed Monthly Costs / (Monthly Rate - Variable Cost per Engagement)

Where:
  Fixed Monthly Costs    = [SaaS + Insurance + Marketing + Legal amortized]
  Monthly Rate           = [Avg engagement rate]
  Variable Cost          = [AI tooling + Travel per engagement]

Given the low fixed-cost structure (founder-led, AI-augmented, no office), break-even is likely achieved with the first billable engagement. The real question is not "can we break even" but "can we generate enough revenue to justify the opportunity cost of founder time vs. alternative pursuits."

6.2 Mitigation Strategies

6.2 Mitigation Strategies

R1 (Capacity bottleneck):

  • Phase 2 network model specifically addresses this
  • Product Org OS amplification extends capacity beyond typical fractional exec
  • Tier structure allows matching engagement intensity to available capacity
  • Document AXIA and Legionis as rigorous, data-backed case studies with specific outcomes
  • Frame 17 years at NICE/Cognyte as the primary credibility source; ProductBeacon engagements as the AI-augmented proof
  • Pursue 1-2 discounted "lighthouse" engagements specifically for case study material
  • Leverage "Leading the Charge" book and thought leadership as authority signals
  • Publish Product Org OS as open-source credibility asset (already done)
  • ProductBeacon's moat is not "uses AI" -- it is the combination of senior judgment + proprietary methodology + purpose-built OS
  • The 82-agent system with Vision to Value integration takes years to replicate
  • Continuously advance the OS (Legionis development feeds back into ProductBeacon capabilities)
  • Maintain minimum 2 concurrent engagements at all times
  • Build pipeline continuously, even when fully utilized
  • Offer lighter-touch advisory retainers alongside deep engagements
  • Anchor pricing against alternatives ($300K+ CPO, $500K+ consulting), not against freelancers
  • Publish ROI framework showing AI-amplified output volume vs. traditional approaches
  • Offer pilot/diagnostic engagements as low-risk entry points
  • Rigorous SOW with defined deliverables per tier
  • Monthly scope reviews built into engagement rhythm
  • Change request process for out-of-scope work
  • Define clear boundary: ProductBeacon = services (human-led), Legionis = platform (self-serve)
  • ProductBeacon engagements seed Legionis product development (feedback loop, not competition)
  • Long-term: ProductBeacon clients become Legionis platform users post-engagement
  • Documentation of all methodologies (already in Product Org OS)
  • Phase 2 network model distributes delivery risk
  • Professional indemnity insurance

9. Decision

9. Decision

Recommendation: GO

Rationale:

  1. The market is real and growing. $5.7B fractional market at 14% growth, AI consulting at 26.2% CAGR. These are not speculative markets -- they are tracked by analysts and supported by structural trends.
  1. The differentiation is defensible. Product Org OS (82 agents, 101 skills) represents years of accumulated methodology and engineering. This is not "a consultant who uses ChatGPT" -- it is a purpose-built operating system for product work. The combination of senior judgment + proprietary AI + proven methodology is genuinely hard to replicate.
  1. The investment is modest. This is a services business, not a product requiring millions in R&D. The core asset (Yohay's expertise + Product Org OS) already exists. Launch investment is primarily brand/website/legal -- manageable within Etsion Brands portfolio.
  1. Two engagements prove feasibility. AXIA ($106K equivalent value in 8 weeks) and Legionis (full E2E build) demonstrate that the model works. The gap is not "can we deliver" but "can we sell."
  1. Strategic synergy with portfolio. ProductBeacon feeds Legionis (services inform product), leverages Maad House (brand/design capabilities), and positions the "Leading the Charge" / Vision to Value thought leadership as commercial assets.

Conditions for GO:

  • ☐ Brand identity and website completed before active outreach
  • ☐ At least one case study (AXIA) formally documented with client approval
  • ☐ Engagement contract reviewed by legal counsel
  • ☐ Pricing validated with 3-5 market conversations before committing to published rates
  • ☐ Clear capacity allocation: how many hours/week dedicated to ProductBeacon vs. other portfolio ventures
