## 1. Pricing Philosophy

### Value-Based, Not Time-Based

ProductBeacon prices on the value delivered, not hours worked. This is a deliberate and foundational choice for three reasons:

**First, the delivery model breaks hourly economics.** When one person with 82 AI agents produces what traditionally requires a 3-5 person consulting team, hourly billing either forces you to charge a rate so high it feels absurd ($1,000+/hour) or so low it leaves massive value on the table. Neither serves the client or the business.

**Second, clients buy outcomes, not time.** A startup CEO doesn't want "20 hours of fractional CPO time." They want a product strategy that gets them to Series A. A scaleup VP Product doesn't want "3 days/week of product leadership." They want a roadmap their team can execute against, and stakeholders aligned around it. Value-based pricing aligns incentives: ProductBeacon is rewarded for delivering more value faster, not for taking longer.

**Third, the AI advantage should benefit both sides.** AI augmentation means ProductBeacon can deliver a competitive analysis in hours instead of weeks. Under hourly billing, the client pays less but ProductBeacon earns less for the same quality output. Under value-based pricing, the client pays for the competitive analysis at its market value, and ProductBeacon captures the efficiency gain. The client still pays far less than alternatives (a consulting firm would bill weeks of analyst time). Both sides win.

### Pricing Principles

| Principle | What It Means |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Price the outcome, not the input** | Engagement pricing reflects deliverable value, not hours consumed |
| **Transparent, not opaque** | Clients know what they're paying and what they're getting before signing |
| **Simple, not clever** | No complex formulas, utilization charges, or hidden fees |
| **Premium, not luxury** | Enterprise-grade quality at mid-market pricing. Not MBB, not freelancer rates |
| **Fair on both sides** | ProductBeacon captures value from AI efficiency; clients pay less than alternatives |

## 2. Pricing Architecture

### Tier 1: Fractional Product Leadership

**Model**: Monthly retainer, renewable quarterly

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Pricing model** | Fixed monthly retainer |
| **Recommended range** | $12,000 - $25,000/month |
| **What's included** | Embedded leadership 2-4 days/week, all AI-augmented deliverables, strategic direction, stakeholder alignment, team mentoring, weekly deliverables |
| **What's extra** | Travel expenses (if required), third-party tool licenses, sub-contracted specialist work |
| **Minimum engagement** | 3 months (with Discovery credit applied) |
| **Payment structure** | Monthly in advance, Net 15 |
| **Renewal** | Quarterly renewal with scope review |

**Price point rationale:**
- A full-time CPO costs $220K-$380K+ in total compensation, or $18K-$32K/month
- ProductBeacon delivers CPO-caliber leadership PLUS team-level execution (AI-augmented)
- At $12K-$25K/month, clients get more output than a full-time CPO at 55-70% of the cost
- The range accommodates scope: 2 days/week at the lower end, 4 days/week at the upper end
- Competitive reference: SaaS Fractional CPO starts at $8K/month (advisory-only, no AI augmentation, no execution output)

**Scope tiers within Fractional Leadership:**

| Level | Days/Week | Monthly | Best For |
|-------|-----------|---------|----------|
| **Strategic** | 2 days | $12,000 - $15,000 | Strategic direction, board prep, key decisions, team mentoring |
| **Operational** | 3 days | $16,000 - $20,000 | Above + roadmap ownership, cross-functional alignment, weekly deliverables |
| **Embedded** | 4 days | $21,000 - $25,000 | Full product leadership, daily involvement, high-throughput delivery |

### Tier 2: Product Build

**Model**: Project-based with milestone payments

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Pricing model** | Fixed project fee, scoped after Discovery |
| **Recommended range** | $30,000 - $80,000 per engagement |
| **What's included** | End-to-end product package: discovery, strategy, requirements, design direction, pricing, GTM, sales enablement, launch coordination |
| **What's extra** | Engineering/development (client-side or referred partner), travel, third-party tools |
| **Minimum engagement** | 6 weeks |
| **Payment structure** | 40% at kickoff, 30% at mid-milestone, 30% at delivery |
| **Scope management** | Change requests scoped and priced separately; minor scope adjustments absorbed |

**Price point rationale:**
- The AXIA engagement delivered ~$106K equivalent value in 8 weeks
- A comparable package from a product studio (Thoughtbot) would cost $150K+ with engineering, or $60-80K for strategy/design only
- MBB firms charge $500K+ for comparable strategic depth (without execution)
- At $30K-$80K, clients get enterprise-quality strategy + execution at 20-50% of agency pricing
- The range reflects engagement complexity: a focused MVP package vs. a full product launch with GTM

**Complexity-based pricing guide:**

| Complexity | Duration | Price Range | Example |
|------------|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Focused** | 6-8 weeks | $30,000 - $40,000 | MVP strategy + requirements for a defined product |
| **Standard** | 8-12 weeks | $40,000 - $60,000 | Full product package: strategy through GTM |
| **Comprehensive** | 12-16 weeks | $60,000 - $80,000 | New market entry: research + strategy + product + GTM + sales enablement |

**Model**: Retainer or project-based, depending on service type

| Element | Detail |

| **What's included** | Varies by service type (see sub-tiers below) |
| **What's extra** | Third-party tools, travel, sub-contracted specialists |
| **Minimum engagement** | Augmentation: 2 months. Transformation: 3 months. Operations: 3 months. |
| **Payment structure** | Retainer: monthly in advance. Project: milestone-based (similar to Tier 2) |

**Sub-tier pricing:**

| Sub-tier | Model | Range | What's Included |
|----------|-------|-------|-----------------|
| **Augmentation** | Monthly retainer | $10,000 - $18,000/mo | Additional PM capacity, product ops, backlog management, sprint coordination |
| **Transformation** | Project fee | $40,000 - $100,000 | Operating model assessment, process redesign, role definition, change management, training |
| **Operations** | Monthly retainer | $12,000 - $20,000/mo | Ongoing product ops, metrics, portfolio management, cross-functional coordination |

**Price point rationale:**
- Augmentation competes with senior contract PM rates ($150-$250/hour x part-time) but delivers more via AI
- Transformation competes with boutique consulting ($200-$500/hour) at a fraction of total cost
- Operations provides ongoing value that would require a full-time hire ($120K-$180K/year) at 65-80% of cost
- PE portfolio application: standardized product practices across investments at a predictable cost per company

## 3. Pricing by Segment

### Startups (Seed to Series B)

**Budget reality**: $5K-$15K/month is typical for fractional leadership. Project budgets of $30K-$80K are possible post-Series A.

| Service | Startup Pricing | Approach |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| **Fractional Leadership** | $12,000 - $15,000/month | Strategic level (2 days/week), 3-month minimum |
| **Product Build** | $30,000 - $50,000 | Focused or Standard complexity, milestone payments |
| **Product Org Services** | Typically N/A | Startups rarely need org transformation yet |
| **Discovery** | $3,000 - $5,000 | 1-week sprint (credited toward engagement) |

**How to make it work for startups:**
- Lead with Discovery ($3K-$5K) as a low-risk entry point
- Offer Fractional Leadership at the Strategic level ($12K/month) for budget-conscious founders
- For Product Build, allow extended milestone payments (e.g., 3 monthly payments instead of 3 lump sums)
- Position against the alternative: a mediocre full-time PM hire ($80K-$120K/year) that delivers less than AI-augmented fractional leadership

### Scaleups (Series B to D)

**Budget reality**: $15K-$30K/month is normal for fractional or consulting engagements. Project budgets of $50K-$150K are standard.

| Service | Scaleup Pricing | Approach |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| **Fractional Leadership** | $16,000 - $25,000/month | Operational or Embedded level, 3-6 month engagement |
| **Product Build** | $40,000 - $80,000 | Standard to Comprehensive complexity |
| **Product Org Services** | $15,000 - $25,000/month or $60K-$100K project | Transformation + ongoing operations |
| **Discovery** | $5,000 - $8,000 | 2-week deep assessment |

**How to position for scaleups:**
- Lead with the value comparison: ProductBeacon at $20K/month vs. full-time CPO at $25K-$32K/month, with more output
- Emphasize the AI-powered execution capacity (scaleups value speed and throughput)
- Offer combined Tier 1 + Tier 3 packages for companies that need both leadership and org improvement
- Reference the AXIA case study ($106K value in 8 weeks) for credibility

### Established Companies

**Budget reality**: $25K-$50K/month for consulting engagements. Project budgets of $100K-$500K for transformation.

| Service | Enterprise Pricing | Approach |
|---------|-------------------|----------|
| **Fractional Leadership** | $20,000 - $25,000/month | Embedded level, 6-12 month engagement |
| **Product Build** | $60,000 - $80,000 | Comprehensive complexity |
| **Product Org Services** | $20,000 - $30,000/month or $80K-$100K project | Full transformation programs |

- Offer executive briefings and board-ready deliverables as standard
- PE/VC portfolio discount: standardized engagement across 3+ portfolio companies at reduced per-company rate

## 4. Entry Point Pricing

### ProductBeacon Discovery

The gateway engagement. Every relationship starts here.

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Price** | $3,000 - $10,000 (segment-dependent, see Section 3) |
| **Duration** | 1-2 weeks |
| **Deliverable** | Discovery Report: current state assessment, gap analysis, opportunity map, engagement recommendation with scope and investment |
| **Credit** | 100% of Discovery fee applied as credit toward the first month of an ongoing engagement |
| **Why it works** | Low financial risk for the client ($3K-$10K vs. committing to $15K+/month), immediate value delivered regardless of next steps, demonstrates ProductBeacon's capability before the big commitment |

**Discovery conversion strategy:**
- The Discovery Report should be impressive enough that the client wants more (demonstrate the AI-augmented delivery speed)
- Include a preliminary deliverable: a competitive landscape draft, a roadmap framework, or a strategic assessment that shows what a full engagement produces
- The 100% credit makes it effectively free if they proceed, removing the "I already paid for Discovery, now I have to pay again?" friction
- Discovery is NOT free. Charging establishes value and filters serious buyers from tire-kickers

### Trial Structure

For clients hesitant about a 3-month commitment:

| Option | Structure | Risk Reduction |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **Discovery** | $3K-$10K, 1-2 weeks, credited toward engagement | Lowest risk, proves capability |
| **One-Month Pilot** | Full monthly rate, 30-day engagement with defined deliverables | One month of real work before committing to 3 months |
| **Scope-Limited Engagement** | Full rate for a defined sub-project (e.g., "competitive landscape + positioning only") | Limits commitment to a specific deliverable |

### Land and Expand Strategy

```
Discovery ($3-10K)
  --> Fractional Leadership 3-month ($12-25K/mo)
    --> Extend to 6-12 months
      --> Add Product Build for specific initiative ($30-80K)
        --> Add Org Services for scaling ($10-30K/mo)
```

Typical expansion pattern:
1. **Land**: Discovery + Fractional Leadership (Tier 1)
2. **First expand**: A Product Build project (Tier 2) surfaces from the strategic work
3. **Second expand**: Product Org Services (Tier 3) to scale what was built
4. **Ongoing**: Advisory retainer post-engagement for continued guidance

## 6. Pricing Mechanics

### Currency

**USD for all engagements.** Even for Israeli or European clients. Reasons:
- Target market is global (US/UK/EU/IL)
- Consulting market prices in USD universally
- Removes currency risk from engagement economics
- Invoicing in USD is standard for B2B professional services

### Billing Cycle

| Engagement Type | Billing |
|-----------------|---------|
| **Retainer (Tier 1, 3)** | Monthly in advance, due on the 1st, Net 15 |
| **Project (Tier 2)** | Milestone-based: 40% kickoff / 30% mid-point / 30% delivery |
| **Discovery** | 100% in advance, before work begins |
| **Advisory (post-engagement)** | Monthly in advance |

### Contract Terms

| Term | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Minimum commitment** | 3 months for retainers; full project term for project-based |
| **Auto-renewal** | Retainers auto-renew quarterly unless either party provides 30-day notice |
| **Scope changes** | Material scope changes require written change order with pricing adjustment |
| **Intellectual property** | Client owns all deliverables. ProductBeacon retains the right to use anonymized methodologies and frameworks |
| **Confidentiality** | Standard mutual NDA. Client data never shared or used for AI training |

### Cancellation and Flexibility

| Scenario | Policy |
|----------|--------|
| **Client cancels within minimum term** | Remaining months billed at 50% of monthly rate |
| **Client cancels after minimum term** | 30-day notice, no penalty |
| **Pause/resume** | One pause of up to 30 days per 12-month period (retainer engagement) |
| **Scale down** | Move to a lower scope tier (e.g., Embedded to Strategic) with 30-day notice |
| **Scale up** | Move to a higher scope tier, effective immediately |

### Scope Management

- Monthly scope review during the first quarterly check-in
- If deliverable volume consistently exceeds the agreed scope, discuss tier upgrade
- Minor scope adjustments (e.g., "can you also look at this competitor?") are absorbed
- Material scope additions (e.g., "can you also build a GTM plan for a second product?") are scoped separately
- The goal is flexibility without scope creep: be generous within reason, firm when boundaries are genuinely exceeded

## 7. Discounting Policy

### When to Discount

| Scenario | Discount | Rationale |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Annual commitment** (12+ months retainer) | 10% off monthly rate | Predictable revenue justifies reduced rate |
| **Multi-tier engagement** (Tier 1 + Tier 2 simultaneously) | 10-15% on combined | Higher total engagement value, deeper relationship |
| **PE/VC portfolio** (3+ portfolio companies) | 15-20% per company | Volume economics, standardized delivery model |
| **Case study rights** | 5-10% off engagement | Marketing value of a public case study justifies discount |
| **Prepayment** (6+ months paid upfront) | 5% off total | Cash flow benefit |

### When NOT to Discount

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| **"Your rate is too high"** | Reframe against alternatives (Section 5). If budget is genuinely constrained, offer a lower scope tier, not a discount on the current tier |
| **"Other fractionals charge $8K/month"** | Differentiate: "They charge $8K because they deliver $8K of value. We charge $15K because we deliver $40K of value with AI augmentation" |
| **"Can you do it cheaper if we sign today?"** | No urgency discounts. Discovery credit ($3-10K) already reduces effective first-month cost |
| **"We're a startup, we can't afford this"** | Offer Discovery + Strategic level ($12K/month). If that's still too much, they're not the right client yet |
| **First engagement** | Never discount the first engagement. This sets the anchor for the entire relationship |

### Discount Governance

- Maximum total discount: 20% (any combination of reasons)
- All discounts documented in the contract with clear rationale
- Discounts are time-bound: annual commitment discount requires annual commitment
- No retroactive discounts
- No "we'll discount now and raise later" -- price the engagement correctly from the start

### When Capacity Expands (Team Growth)

As ProductBeacon adds practitioners:
- Each practitioner uses the Product Org OS methodology and AI agents
- Yohay oversees quality and leads strategic direction
- Pricing stays the same or increases (the brand commands the rate, not the individual)
- Each practitioner can handle 2-3 concurrent engagements
- Margin expands: practitioner cost < client billing rate

### Price Increase Protocol

- Annual review of all pricing tiers
- Existing clients grandfathered for current term, new rate at renewal
- Increases tied to demonstrated value (more case studies, higher NPS, proven outcomes)
- Maximum annual increase: 15% (unless market conditions dramatically change)
- Always communicate increase with value justification, never as a cost-of-living adjustment
