## 1. GTM Approach: Founder-Led Content-to-Trust Motion

### The Motion

ProductBeacon's GTM is **founder-led sales, fueled by content-driven inbound and referral activation**. This is not PLG (we sell services, not software). This is not enterprise sales (no BDR team, no 6-month cycles). This is a trust-based motion where Yohay's credibility, visible proof of work, and network convert into engagements.

### Why This Motion

**The logic chain:**

1. **Services businesses sell trust.** A startup CEO hiring a fractional CPO is making a deeply personal bet. They need to believe this person understands their world, has done it before, and will deliver. No amount of ad spend replaces that.

2. **Yohay's profile IS the differentiator.** 17 years at NICE and Cognyte, $200M+ portfolios, 30+ person orgs, a published book on product org management, and an open-source AI system with 82 agents. This profile doesn't need to be manufactured — it needs to be made visible.

3. **AI augmentation changes the unit economics.** Traditional fractional CPOs sell time. ProductBeacon sells outcomes amplified by AI. This means the content strategy can show, not just tell — live demos, real deliverables, open-source code. That's a moat competitors can't replicate quickly.

4. **The buyer journey is short but trust-intensive.** A Seed-B founder sees a LinkedIn post or hears Yohay on a podcast. They check the profile, read a case study, explore the OS. Within days they're on a discovery call. The GTM motion must compress trust-building into that window.

### Why NOT Other Motions

| Motion | Why Not |
|--------|---------|
| Product-Led Growth | No self-serve product to adopt. Product Org OS is a lead magnet, not the revenue engine. |
| Enterprise Sales | Too heavy. Requires BDRs, AEs, SEs, and long cycles. ProductBeacon is a one-person-plus-AI operation. |
| Channel/Reseller | Premature. No brand equity yet to attract channel partners at scale. |
| Paid-first | High CAC for a services business with [TBD] price points. Paid is Phase 3, not Phase 1. |
| Outbound-first | Cold email for fractional CPO services has poor conversion. Trust must precede the ask. |

### The Flywheel

```
Content (proof of work)
    → Visibility (LinkedIn, speaking, OS)
        → Inbound interest (DMs, referrals, website)
            → Discovery call
                → Engagement
                    → Results (new proof of work)
                        → Content (case study, teardown)
                            → [repeat]
```

Every engagement produces new proof of work, which becomes new content, which generates new leads. This is the core engine.

## 4. Sales Process

### Lead Qualification (BANT-Adapted for Services)

| Criteria | Qualified | Not Qualified |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| **Budget** | Can invest in fractional/advisory product leadership | Looking for free advice or very early pre-seed with no budget |
| **Authority** | CEO, CTO, or board member involved | Mid-level PM looking for mentorship (redirect to coaching) |
| **Need** | Actively building/scaling product org, or product strategy gap | "Just exploring" with no timeline |
| **Timing** | Need help within 1-3 months | "Maybe next year" |
| **Fit** | B2B SaaS/tech, product-centric business | Hardware, pure services, non-tech |

### Lead Scoring Signals

| Signal | Score | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Inbound via referral | High | Network |
| Downloaded OS / starred GitHub | Medium | GitHub |
| Engaged with 3+ LinkedIn posts | Medium | LinkedIn |
| Attended webinar | Medium | Registration |
| Cold inbound via website | Medium-Low | Website form |
| Responded to selective outreach | Low-Medium | LinkedIn DM |

### Discovery Call Structure (30 minutes)

| Phase | Time | Purpose |
|-------|------|---------|
| **Context** | 5 min | Understand their company, stage, funding, team size |
| **Pain** | 10 min | What's the specific product challenge? What have they tried? What's the cost of not solving it? |
| **Vision** | 5 min | Where do they want to be in 6-12 months? What does success look like? |
| **Fit Assessment** | 5 min | Present relevant experience (AXIA for strategy gaps, Legionis for build, OS for org scaling). Gauge reaction. |
| **Next Steps** | 5 min | If fit: propose a scoping session. If not: recommend alternatives. Always leave value (share a framework, connect them with someone). |

**Critical rule:** Never pitch on the discovery call. Diagnose. The pitch comes in the proposal, backed by the diagnosis.

### Proposal Format

Every proposal follows a consistent structure:

1. **Situation Summary** — What we discussed, what the challenge is (proves listening)
2. **Recommended Approach** — Which service tier fits, what the engagement looks like
3. **Scope & Deliverables** — Specific outputs they'll receive (using Vision to Value phases)
4. **Timeline** — Phased plan with milestones
5. **Investment** — Pricing per the relevant tier
6. **Why ProductBeacon** — Brief proof points (AXIA metrics, OS, experience)
7. **Next Steps** — Sign, kick off date, onboarding

**Format:** Clean, branded PDF. Not a slide deck. Not a wall of text. 3-5 pages max.

### Close Process

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| Discovery call | Day 0 | Qualify, diagnose, align |
| Proposal sent | Day 1-3 | Same week as discovery call |
| Follow-up | Day 5-7 | One check-in, no pressure |
| Decision | Day 7-14 | If no decision in 14 days, ask for a direct yes/no |
| Contract | Day 14-21 | Simple services agreement (not a 30-page MSA) |
| Kickoff | Day 21-28 | Onboarding session, context download, first sprint plan |

**Discovery-to-kickoff target:** 3-4 weeks for startups (they move fast). May stretch to 6-8 weeks for larger scaleups.

### Contract Principles

- **Simple agreement**: 2-3 pages. Scope, timeline, fees, IP, termination.
- **Monthly billing**: Invoice at start of each month. Net 14 days.
- **Minimum commitment**: 3 months for fractional leadership (meaningful results take time). Month-to-month for advisory.
- **Exit clause**: Either party, 30 days notice. No lock-in beyond 30 days.
- **IP**: Client owns all deliverables. ProductBeacon retains methodology and AI tools.
