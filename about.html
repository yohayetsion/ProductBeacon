ABOUT PRODUCTBEACON

The practice that installs and governs an AI workforce

Anyone can spin up AI agents. Almost no one can govern them. ProductBeacon exists to close that gap: we install a full AI workforce inside your business and give you the know-how to run it, or we run it for you. The same stack our founder runs his own companies on.

The person behind the practice

ProductBeacon was founded by Yohay Etsion, who spent 17 years building and leading product organizations at NICE and Cognyte, managing portfolios north of $200M and teams of 30+.

At NICE, Yohay led product management for enterprise security platforms serving Fortune 500 companies. At Cognyte (formerly Verint's cyber intelligence division), he ran the product organization through a public listing and beyond.

That experience revealed what works at scale: how to build product strategy that survives contact with reality, how to structure teams that ship consistently, and how to make decisions under pressure with incomplete information.

Today Yohay runs his own businesses on a governed AI workforce, and ProductBeacon installs and operates that same workforce for clients. The discipline of a real product organization, compressed into a stack one operator can command.

Why ProductBeacon exists

After nearly two decades inside large product organizations, a pattern became clear: companies need the output of a real organization, but the talent pool is thin, expensive, and slow to hire. Then AI agents arrived, and a different gap opened up.

Platforms will sell you agents all day long. None of them sell you the one thing that makes agents safe to use: the ability to govern what they produce. Which agent for which job, what to check before you trust it, where a human has to stay in the loop. That is the entire distance between "I have AI tools" and "I run an AI workforce."

ProductBeacon closes that distance two ways. The Operator Intensive installs the workforce inside your business in one day and hands you the know-how to govern it yourself. ProductBeacon On Call runs the workforce for you, the same practice, one stage on. Either way, you get a governed AI workforce, not a pile of agents and a guess.

And none of it is improvised. The operating method is written down: in the Vision to Value book and formalized in the Decision Provenance Standard. We run real businesses on it, our founder's own and our clients', before we ever hand it to you.
