How On Call works

It is the Operator Intensive's third stage, offered on its own. You do not have to learn to operate the stack first. On anything that carries legal, financial, or compliance weight, a qualified human stays in the loop.

It is the same practice the Intensive opens onto, one stage on. If you ever decide you would rather run it yourself, the Operator Intensive is the door back.
