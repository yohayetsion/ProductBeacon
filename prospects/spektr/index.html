04
YOUR MOAT

Architecture, Cross-Regime Proof, and an Emerging Data Asset

Composable Agent Architecture

Individual AI agents (KYB, Doc Review, Network Discovery) that combine into custom processes. Enterprise buyers start narrow and expand, reducing procurement risk. The custom agent builder extends this into a platform play no competitor matches.

Cross-Regime Deployment

Phantom (crypto, 20M+ users) alongside Santander Leasing (traditional banking) demonstrates agents that work across fundamentally different regulatory environments. This is a platform characteristic, not a workflow tool characteristic.

Compliance Decision Data

Every document reviewed, every beneficial owner mapped, every risk scored becomes training data. If spektr builds a feedback loop where accuracy improves with usage, it creates a moat no competitor can replicate without equivalent deployment scale.

THE NARRATIVE

08
RECOMMENDATIONS

Four Moves

  1. Reframe from Infrastructure to Outcomes

    Replace "agent infrastructure" with compliance outcomes: fewer false positives, faster onboarding, audit readiness. The KYB AI page should open with "missed beneficial owner structures," not "composable agent architecture."

  2. Build the Santander Proof Narrative

    Commission a detailed case study that becomes the centerpiece of every go-to-market motion. Structure: compliance challenge at scale, why existing tools failed, how agents performed, measurable outcomes. This single asset is worth more than a redesigned homepage.

  3. Launch a Content Strategy

    Publish a quarterly "AI Compliance Benchmark." Write the definitive guide to AI agents in regulated industries. Create segment-specific content for banks vs. fintechs vs. crypto. The narrative vacuum will not stay empty.

  4. Force the Identity Choice

    Compliance workflow tool (solid, capped ceiling) or AI agent platform for regulated industries (higher ceiling, harder execution)? The custom agent builder hints at platform. The absence of a developer ecosystem says otherwise. Choose before the $20M gets spread across both.

09
THE OPPORTUNITY

The Bigger Picture

The AI compliance market is in a land-grab phase where four well-funded competitors are racing to convert identical vocabulary into enterprise traction. spektr has the strongest enterprise proof point (Santander), the most modular architecture, and deployment across traditional and crypto regulatory regimes.

But the window is defined by competitor velocity. Duna's larger round buys more runway. Every quarter without spektr claiming the narrative, the category story gets written by someone else.

The compliance decision data accumulating in spektr's agents is the asset that will determine who wins in three years. Whether spektr captures and compounds that data deliberately, or lets it accumulate passively, is the product leadership question that defines the trajectory.
