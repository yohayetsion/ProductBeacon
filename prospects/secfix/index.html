YOUR MOAT

Two Proof Points Nobody Else Claims, One Emerging Asset

100% Audit Success Rate

In a trust-dependent market where companies bet their compliance certification on the platform's guidance, a perfect audit record is the strongest possible proof point. No other EU compliance platform makes this claim publicly. It should anchor every piece of marketing.

100% Customer Satisfaction

Combined with audit success, this credibility pair answers the buyer's core question: "Will this actually get me through the audit?" These are not vanity numbers. They are competitive weapons sitting on the About page instead of the homepage.

CISO AI as Emerging Moat

If the AI genuinely understands multiple compliance frameworks across EU jurisdictions, trained on real audit data from hundreds of customers, it creates an intelligence advantage that deepens with every engagement. The 90% automation claim suggests the platform handles most tasks. CISO AI needs to deliver the judgment-heavy remaining 10%.

08
RECOMMENDATIONS

Four Moves

  1. Lead with the Audit Success Rate

    Move "100% audit success rate" from the About page to the homepage hero. Build the entire positioning around this claim. When a buyer asks "why Secfix?" the answer should be immediate: "Every customer has passed every audit."

  2. Run Framework Prioritization

    Identify which of 10 frameworks drive acquisition, which drive retention, which are table stakes. Concentrate engineering on the frameworks that win deals. Move the rest to maintenance. Vanta proved depth in two frameworks can build a billion-dollar company.

  3. Elevate the Founding Team

    Forbes CEO, Amazon CTO, 15-year CISO. In a trust-dependent market, this team is the proof a 50-person startup delivers enterprise-grade outcomes. Stop treating them as About page biographies. Weave them into every go-to-market motion.

  4. Define CISO AI Strategy with Milestones

    The AI launch competes for engineering resources with framework coverage and reliability. Build a roadmap with specific targets: "handles 50% of ISO 27001 audit prep without intervention by Q3" is a strategy. "AI-powered assistant" is a feature announcement.

09
THE OPPORTUNITY

The Bigger Picture

Secfix has achieved something remarkable: a perfect audit record across every customer engagement. In a market where the entire purchase decision hinges on trust, that record is the most valuable asset the company owns.

The $12M creates both opportunity and pressure. The temptation to expand is strong when capital is fresh. But the competitive landscape rewards depth over breadth. DataGuard is going deep on the German Mittelstand. Orbiq is owning NIS2 and DORA. Vanta is expanding into Europe with massive resources.

The question is not whether Secfix can cover ten frameworks. It already does. The question is whether it can be the undisputed best at three of them before the competition catches up on the rest. Framework prioritization is not a product management exercise. It is the strategic decision that determines whether the $12M builds a category leader or funds a feature war nobody wins.
